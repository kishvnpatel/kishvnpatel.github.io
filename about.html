I am a filmmaker who loves to create art. I've made a couple short films already and always have another on the horizon. 

I film on a variety of different cameras, but my favorite to film on is an old camcorder. The videos come out low quality and it's a more run-and-gun type thing, but there's a certain feel to them that I like.

I like to capture moments in my life and have been documenting my life through film for a while now and I edit them together to create a string of memories for my friends and I to watch. They're a part of my collection of 'videotapes,' inspired by the look and feel of home videos. I think it's nice to have your memories kept somewhere other than your head. 

I work in a variety of different mediums that can be seen across my page. I film, edit, write, record, really just anything. I can't see myself doing anything else.

The dream is to eventually direct and write my own movies!
